PvP Gear and Stats

Gear plays a vital role in PvP success. Choosing the right gear and stat priorities based on your class and spec can provide you with a significant advantage in combat. Here are some essential tips for gearing up:

  • Item Level: Higher item levels generally provide more health and damage potential, so aim to equip the best available gear for your spec.
  • Versatility: This stat increases both your damage and healing output while reducing incoming damage. It's one of the most important stats for PvP.
  • Critical Strike: The more critical strikes you land, the higher your potential for burst damage. Focus on items with a higher Critical Strike rating if you prefer aggressive playstyles.
  • Mastery: Depending on your class and spec, Mastery can increase your damage, healing, or survivability. Mastery-based builds are often favored in sustained fights.
