Top PvP Classes for The War Within 11.0.7

World of Warcraft's PvP landscape is constantly shifting, with every patch comes changes to the balance of power between classes. Here are the top-performing classes and specs for PvP in the current patch.

1. Discipline Priest

Discipline Priests excel in PvP due to their ability to heal and deal damage simultaneously. They rely on managing Atonement, where healing spells heal allies and simultaneously deal damage to enemies. This spec is extremely strong in arena settings where both offensive and defensive support is needed.

  • Strengths: High utility, strong healing, and damage from Atonement. Ability to outplay enemy healers with damage and crowd control. Psychic Scream and Mind Dominate are powerful CC's and need to be used regularly.
  • Weaknesses: Requires a high skill cap to maximize effectiveness, vulnerable to heavy burst damage and crowd control. It is difficult to maintain DPS (DamagePerSecond) while keeping allys healed.
  • Current Builds I am currently using Oracle for 3's and BG content. I also use it for 2's matches with oppoents being double DPS. The Voidweaver build with mindbender is best when doing 2's matches against opposing teams that have a healer, as the mind bender build can preserve mana more effectively while the Oracle build can pump out more heals per second (HPS).

2. Balance Druid

Balance Druids are a versatile ranged class with great mobility and strong burst potential. Their ability to deal AoE damage and spread crowd control through Solar Beam and Entangling Roots makes them highly effective in both arenas and battlegrounds.

  • Strengths: High burst potential with Incarnation:Chosen of Elune and Starsurge, strong utility with roots and AoE damage. Has the advantage of starting combat in catform while in Stealth, catching opponents off guard. Has Bear Form, which gives the advantage of Bash and Frenzied Regeneration.
  • Weaknesses: Squishy, requires good positioning and awareness to maximize effectiveness.
  • Current Build: Focus on maximizing Solar Eclipse for increased damage from Starsurge. Use Moonfire and Sunfire to spread damage and keep pressure on enemies. Incorporate Solar Beam for interrupting enemy casters and Typhoon to peel off melee.

3. Retribution Paladin

Retribution Paladins are a powerhouse in PvP, capable of high burst damage and strong utility. Their ability to heal themselves with Word of Glory and sustain through defensive cooldowns makes them formidable opponents in arenas.

  • Strengths: High burst potential with Ashes of the Wake and Hammer of Light while casting fully charged Templar's Verdict, self-sustain with Word of Glory, and strong crowd control.
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable when cooldowns are down, susceptible to crowd control and interrupts.
  • Current Build: Focus on using the passive version of Avenging Wrath by casting Ashes of the Wake for burst damage during key moments. Utilize Divine Shield and Bubble Hearth for survivability. Keep up with Judgment and Templar's Verdict for consistent damage output.

4. Marksmanship Hunter

Marksmanship Hunters are a ranged DPS spec that excels in both burst damage and kiting. Their ability to control the fight from range while maintaining high uptime on damage through Chimaera Shot and Aimed Shot makes them highly effective in PvP.

  • Strengths: High burst potential with Rapid Fire and Aimed Shot, excellent mobility, and great range.
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to heavy melee pressure and crowd control.
  • Current Build: Focus on maximizing burst with Trueshot and using Aimed Shot for hard-hitting damage. Use Exhilaration to sustain yourself and Hunter's Mark to increase damage on your target.

5. Beast Mastery Hunter

Beast Mastery Hunters are strong PvP contenders due to their pet utility and versatility. Their ability to have their pets attack from afar while maintaining their own mobility is perfect for both 2v2 and 3v3 scenarios.

  • Strengths: High sustained damage from pets, mobility, great for kiting.
  • Weaknesses: Pets can be easily killed, lower burst potential compared to other ranged classes.
  • Current Build: Focus on maximizing pet damage with Dire Beast, Call of the Wild, and Bestial Wrath for increased damage output. Use Kill Command frequently to maintain constant pressure.

6. Windwalker Monk

Windwalker Monks excel in PvP thanks to their high mobility, crowd control, and sustained damage. Their ability to quickly move around the battlefield and deal with enemies through high-damage abilities like Fists of Fury and Rising Sun Kick make them deadly in close-quarters combat.

  • Strengths: Great mobility, high sustained damage, strong crowd control with Leg Sweep
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to being kited or stunned.
  • Current Build: Focus on utilizing Fists of Fury for AoE damage, Rising Sun Kick for burst, and Leg Sweep for crowd control. Maintain uptime with Touch of Death for finishing blows.

7. Mistweaver Monk

Mistweaver Monks are hybrid healers in PvP that can use a combination of powerful heals, damage, and crowd control. Their ability to transition between healing and dealing damage makes them unique in any PvP setting.

  • Strengths: High mobility, great healing throughput, strong utility with Leg Sweep and Renewing Mist.
  • Weaknesses: Lower damage output compared to other DPS, requires excellent positioning.
  • Current Build: Focus on using Soothing Mist for healing while using Fistweaving for increased damage and healing. Utilize Zen Pulse for AoE healing and Life Cocoon for big heals.

8. Affliction Warlock

Affliction Warlocks excel at controlling enemies with damage over time (DoT) effects. They can pressure enemies over long fights and use crowd control to lock down enemies while dealing substantial damage.

  • Strengths: High sustained damage with Agony and Unstable Affliction, excellent crowd control with Fear.
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to burst damage if not well-positioned.
  • Current Build: The Affliction Warlock focuses on maximizing sustained damage through key abilities like Agony Unstable Affliction and Corruption, while maintaining crowd control with Fear and Mortal Coil. Survivability is enhanced through Drain Life and Unending Resolve for defensive cooldowns.

9. Frost Mage

Frost Mages excel at controlling the battlefield with powerful crowd control, kiting enemies, and consistent burst damage. Their primary focus is on slowing enemies while dealing damage with abilities like FrostBolt and IceLance.

  • Strengths: Strong control abilities like Frost Nova and Polymorph provide excellent crowd control, freezing or incapacitating enemies for easy follow-ups. Excellent survivability, Ice Block and Blink allow them to escape danger and reset fights quickly. High burst potential when paired with Frozen Orb and Icy Veins, Frost Mages can deal significant burst damage.
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to melee, Frost Mages struggle against high-mobility melee classes who can close the gap quickly. Low sustained damage: Unlike other specs, Frost Mages are highly reliant on burst phases, which can leave them underwhelming over long periods.
  • Current Build: Key abilities: Frostbolt, Ice Lance, Frozen Orb, and Icy Veins are the backbone of Frost Mage damage. Cooldowns like Ice Block are great for defensive play and Blizzard for area control Talent Choices: Glacial Spike for strong burst damage and Shatter for maximizing Ice Lance damage.

10. Fury Warrior

Fury Warriors excel at dealing sustained damage with rapid attacks using two-handed weapons. Their aggressive playstyle is centered around high burst damage, mobility, and high uptime on their target with abilities like Rampage and Colossus Smash.

  • Strengths: High burst damage with Rampage and Colossus Smash makes them a constant threat. Their mobility through Charge and Heroic Leap allows them to close gaps quickly and maintain pressure. They also have strong defensive abilities like Die by the Sword and Enraged Regeneration, providing great survivability in intense situations.
  • Weaknesses: Fury Warriors are vulnerable to crowd control and can struggle when locked down. They also rely heavily on key cooldowns like Rampage and Battle Cry, making them less effective when these abilities are unavailable. Their sustained damage can be lower than other classes when not using cooldowns effectively.
  • Current Build: Key abilities: Rampage, Colossus Smash, and Execute are central to Fury Warrior's damage. Cooldowns like Battle Cry and Die by the Sword are essential for maximizing both offense and defense. Talent Choices: Sudden Death to increase Execute damage and War Machine to boost mobility and rage generation.

11. Havoc Demon Hunter

Havoc Demon Hunters are fast, mobile, and deal high sustained damage while being incredibly difficult to pin down. Their playstyle focuses on hitting hard with abilities like Chaos Strike and Eye Beam while utilizing incredible mobility to stay on top of their targets.

  • Strengths: Havoc Demon Hunters are incredibly mobile with abilities like Vengeful Retreat and Fel Rush allowing them to close gaps or escape danger. Their damage is highly consistent thanks to Chaos Strike and their ability to quickly reset abilities like Eye Beam. They also have strong self-healing capabilities through Spectral Sight and Leech.
  • Weaknesses: While they have high mobility, Havoc Demon Hunters are somewhat squishy and can struggle when caught in heavy crowd control. They rely on key cooldowns like Metamorphosis to maximize their damage, making them less effective when those abilities are unavailable. Their sustained damage output can also feel lacking in longer fights if cooldowns are not properly managed.
  • Current Build: Key abilities: Chaos Strike, Eye Beam, and Blade Dance are central to Havoc Demon Hunter's burst and sustained damage. Cooldowns like Metamorphosis and The Hunt increase overall damage and survivability. Talent Choices: First Blood for single-target damage, Unbound Chaos for AoE burst, and Fury of the Illidari for additional burst potential.
